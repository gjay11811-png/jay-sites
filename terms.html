1. The service

JG Studio designs and builds websites for businesses. Each project's scope, price and delivery estimate are agreed in writing (usually WhatsApp or email) before work begins.

2. Payment

Projects are billed 50% up front and 50% on your approval of the finished site. The final balance is only due once you've confirmed you're happy. Free previews are exactly that — free, with no obligation.

3. Delivery

The 48-hour build window applies to Starter Sites and starts when the deposit is received and your content (name, photos, prices) is provided. Larger builds have timelines agreed per project.

4. Ownership

On full payment, you own the finished website, its content and the domain registered for it — completely. You're free to move, modify or rehost it at any time. No lock-ins.

5. Revisions

Every build includes 30 days of free reasonable tweaks after launch. Ongoing changes beyond that are covered by the optional Care Plan or quoted individually.

6. Content responsibility

You confirm that any content you supply (photos, text, logos) is yours to use. JG Studio does not build sites for illegal products or services, including counterfeit goods.

7. Care Plan

The Care Plan is a monthly rolling service — cancel any time with no notice period. Cancelling ends hosting management; your site and files remain yours.
