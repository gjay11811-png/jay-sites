Questions

Asked all the time

Why so much cheaper than agencies?

No office, no account managers, no 6-week timelines. I design and build directly using modern tools — you get agency-quality work without paying for agency overheads. Right now I'm also offering founding-client pricing for my first 5 projects, in exchange for a review and permission to feature your site.

What do you need from me?

Your business name, phone number, a few photos and roughly what you offer. That's it — I write the words and handle everything technical.

Do I really own it?

Yes — the site, domain and content are 100% yours. No lock-ins. If you ever want to leave, you take everything with you.

What about hosting and the domain?

I set both up for you. Hosting is included free on the Care Plan, or I'll show you the simple way to run it yourself (about £10/year for a domain).

Can you do online shops?

Yes — full stores with card payments, product pages and order notifications. See First Class Fits in the work section for a live example.
